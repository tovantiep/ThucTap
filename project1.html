Ha Long Bay
Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and popular travel destination in Quảng Ninh Province, Vietnam. The name Hạ Long means "descending dragon" Ha Long Bay has about 1,960–2,000 islets.

Himalaya
The Himalayas are a mountain range in Asia that separates the Indian subcontinent from the Tibetan plateau. The name is derived from Sanskrit himālaya, a tatpurusa compound word meaning "abode of snow".
